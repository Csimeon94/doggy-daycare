SERVICES WE PROVIDE

DAYCARE

My daycare offers a safe and fun environment where your dog children are the top priority! Pets get that at-home comfort while being able to interact with other furry friends. We specialize in small dogs 40 lbs and under so the little guys & gals can play worry free! Apologies, but aggressive dogs are not welcome - meet & greets are a must.

OVERNIGHT STAY

Need someone to watch your furry friend? My Daycare offers overnight stays. With comfortable crates and an overnight supervisor to make sure your pet is taken care of. you can rest easy knowing your dog is in good hands.

RETAIL STORE

We get it,sometimes life gets you a bit busy. Sometimes there are days you are running around like a chicken with its head cut off. Well rest easy knowing that my daycare has a retail center. Thats right, you need dog food? New toys? A frozen puppy treat for those hot days? You can call us and pick and choose what treat you want to buy.

GROOMING

Did your furry friend decide walking through a mud hole was much better than avoiding it? Well dont worry, we have groomers that can take care of getting your pet squeaky clean.

TRAINING

Training is important and not everyone has the time for it. With our certified dog trainers, we cant make sure your dog has the best training possible.after a few weeks your dog will be the most behaved dog in the neighborhood.

VACCINE CLINIC

It is important to us that your little furry friend is happy and healthy. We have trained vets to see to it that your pet is happy and healthy. We can take care of any vaccines your pet may need!
